Difference between red and blue knots

  • With the BEST alignment we have
    • Red knots: t = 6.7 +/- 1.6 kyr (total range 4 → 10)
    • Blue knots: t = 1.6 +/- 0.2 kyr (total range 1 → 4)
    • Mean difference: dt = 5.1 +/- 1.6 => larger than 3 sigma
    • No time overlap of ejections
  • With the SHIFT alignment
    • Red knots: t = 3.7 +/- 0.5 kyr (total range 3 → 4)
    • Blue knots: t = 2.3 +/- 0.3 kyr (total range 1 → 4)
    • Mean difference: dt = 1.4 +/- 0.6 kyr => larger than 2 sigma
    • Possible time overlap of ejections
      • SW Faint Blue is contemporaneous with the Red knots
        • but this is also the least certain measurement
      • The red knots may easily all have been ejected at the same time (roughly 3500 yrs ago)
      • The blue knots are marginally consistent with a common ejection time of about 2000 yrs ago
